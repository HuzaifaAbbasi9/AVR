How it works

The AVR Money App is very simple to use. Getting some extra money really is as easy as 1, 2, and 3.

Earn Credit

Complete as many tasks as you can in “Hot Offers” tab. The tasks vary and can be watching videos, sharing, completing surveys and many more. We recommend checking the offers stated as “hot” as update often and provide high credits.

Cash-out

Once your Credits balance (displayed on the top right of the screen) is enough to cash-out you can do so at the “Payouts” tab by requesting a pay-out to be sent to your Paypal account or bank card.

Enjoy your money

Payouts take around 48 hours and are paid directly to your account. We notify you once the payout is completed and then you can enjoy your money.
